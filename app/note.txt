codes for cell state:

0 - empty_cell
1 - cell contains player 1's piece
2 - cell contains player 2's piece
3 - cell contains player 1's piece and is selected
4 - cell contains player 2's piece and is selected
5 - cell contains player 1's piece and is part of a hit!
6 - cell contains player 2's piece and is part of a hit!

note: A hit happens when 3 pieces are aligned in either the horizontal or vertical axis
